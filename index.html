Carnelian

A calm app for focused writing

Carnelian is a calm, distraction-free writing app for people who need to get words on the page before they get lost in revision. Typewriter mode keeps you moving. Themes keep it beautiful. And when you're ready, compile your chapters into a finished manuscript.

Stay in the draft

Typewriter mode locks the cursor to the end of your document. You can only write forward — no going back to fuss with that sentence in paragraph two. It might sound like a constraint, but it's crucial for finishing your draft: write messily, keep going, and finish your first (or final!) draft. Turn on focus mode and everything except the paragraph you're working on fades away. Just you and the sentence in front of you.

Screenshot: Typewriter mode and focus mode active, with the current paragraph highlighted and surrounding text dimmed

Your space, your atmosphere

Whether you prefer a paper-like page or want a dark screen for 3AM writing sessions, Carnelian ships with six built-in themes and supports custom theming. Pick the one that makes you want to sit down and write.

Screenshot: Three themes side by side — Carnelian, Dracula, and Mint Choc Chip

From chapters to manuscript

Write each chapter as its own markdown file. When it's time to pull it all together, open the compile window, choose what to include, and export the whole thing as a single docx or .md document. One click takes you from a folder of scattered chapters to a finished manuscript — ready to share, print, or submit.

Screenshot: The compile window with chapter files selected and export options visible

Local and private

Your files are plain markdown, in ordinary folders on your computer. Carnelian was designed to be simple and focused, with no account linking or wi-fi requirement. Open a folder. Write. That's it.

Ready to write?

Carnelian runs on Mac, Windows, and Linux. It operates on a 'pay what you like' model, with donations supporting continued development.
