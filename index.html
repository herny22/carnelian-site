Why Carnelian?

I designed Carnelian due to my own experiences as a writer with ADHD. No matter how much you love it, writing can be hard work, so distraction or procrastination can easily get in the way. Most writing tools tie you in to an account, a particular workflow, or a subscription — I wanted to create a focused editor that can be used anywhere, as part of any workflow, for free. Thanks for trying it out.

— Henry
